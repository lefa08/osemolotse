QUARTERLY HYDRAULIC SERVICES

Africa Hydro Rep Solutions offers hydraulic and load body services at our normal rates, in order to ensure that your vehicles always operate at optimal capacity. These services are provided on a quarterly basis to maintain the integrity and reliability of your hydraulic and load body systems. Our team of experienced technicians will inspect, diagnose, and repair any malfunctions in order to ensure that your vehicles remain safe and operational.

We offer comprehensive hydraulic services, including:

  • The replacement of all hydraulic filters
  • Cleaning of all suction strainers
  • Inspection of all grease nipples and replacement where necessary
  • Preparation of hydraulic oils and resetting of pressure, and detailed system checks and reports
  • Additionally, we inspect all hydraulic cylinders
  • Loose mounting clips, and repair where necessary
  • Inspect the pins and bushes for excessive wear
  • Inspect the guides and slides for excessive wear
  • Inspect PTOs and pumps for potential damage and leaks
  • Inspect load bodies for cracks and other damages
  • Inspect the tailgate for damages and possible excess wear of the hopper floor and sweeper blade
  • And check all hoses for signs of wear and tear

WEEKLY VEHICLE FLEET SERVICES

During the weekly inspection of your fleet, the following items will be checked, and you will be advised on areas that need attention:

What we believe in:

  • Inspecting all hydraulic cylinders for leaks and ram damages
  • Inspecting hydraulic piping for leaks and loose mounting clips
  • Measuring the cycle times
  • Checking stabiliser operation on cherry pickers and truck-mounted cranes
  • Inspecting the structure of cherry pickers and validity of their safety certificates
  • Checking the condition of cables and ropes
  • Inspecting truck-mounted crane stability and certificate health
  • Inspecting and greasing the rooster sheave, jib sheaves, and jib winches.

A detailed report regarding any damage, misuse, or abuse of equipment will be submitted to the client. The report will provide an indication of the damage/breakages that must be addressed urgently, the estimated downtime for repairs, and the consequences and seriousness that would result if the damage and/or breakage is not attended to.
