About Us

Who We Are

Osemolotse is a firm that specializes in various services and projects. Our company specializes with these services in domestic, provincial, national and international markets based within South Africa. The firm provides these services using technologically superior processes, providing greater value for clients and enhanced design coupled with project management and maintenance. Our financial model is based on government providing sovereign guarantee or form a commercial source in the event of payment default.

Keys to Success

  • Provide a service that is technologically superior to the client
  • Utilizing a diverse staff of consultants to provide a wide variety of product styles.
  • A large array of national and global connections and networking.

Our Goals

Osemolotse is a formidable privately held holding firm with an ever- increasing global reach and client base. Among its portfolio of clients are legions of government, non-governmental, and privately owned entities representing Africa and Europe.

The group strives to meet the needs of any business that requests its assistance, thus culminating a broad range of services including financial and non-financial. The companies complete portfolio of businesses spread across various sectors of the economy including, construction, infrastructure, environment & energy, leasing, PPP and BOT projects, healthcare, financial services, amongst others.

In order to accommodate a seemingly boundless formation of clients, the company’s associates are thoughtfully paired with suitable patrons and tailor each project to suit distinct needs.

As a result Osemolotse team's tireless fidelity to its businesses and customers, it has seen significant growth since 2005 and is highly regarded as an industry forerunner in some sectors of the economy.
