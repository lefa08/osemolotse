About Us

Who We Are

Africa Hydro Rep Solutions is a leading hydraulics company that has been providing high-quality and reliable products and services for the past four years.

We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience, products, and services. Our team is dedicated to providing the highest quality services at competitive prices.

At Africa Hydro Rep Solutions, we have extensive experience in all aspects of hydraulics, ranging from design and fabrication to installation and maintenance. We specialize in a wide range of hydraulic systems and components, including hydraulic components on all earth-mounted vehicles, earth-mounted equipment, and underground equipment.

Additionally, we also deal in specialized bodies and UFTIMS mechanisms, development, installation, and maintenance of hydraulic systems on various types of specialized vehicles such as environmental trucks, wast compactors, waste compactor semi-trailers, skip coaters, hook lifts, hydraulic tipper garbage trucks, refuse container washing and disinfecting vehicles, road sweepers, canal sewage vehicles, transportation trucks, side tippers, special products and all machinery, construction equipment, honey suckers, and combination vacuum tankers

Moreover, we also do repairs on all truck-mounted hydraulics and bodies, hydraulic counters and power packs, and all earth-moving buckets. Our experienced technicians are knowledgeable in all types of hydraulic systems and can provide expert advice on the best solution for your needs. We believe in providing the highest level of customer service and satisfaction. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible product, service, and support. Our team is always available to answer any questions and provide guidance on the best solution for your needs.We take great pride in being one of the leading hydraulics companies in the industry.

We look forward to working with you to meet your needs and exceed your expectations. Thank you for considering Africa Hydro Rep Solutions for all your hydraulic needs.
