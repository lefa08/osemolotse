HEALTH SAFETY & ENVIRONMENT (HSE)

The Health and Safety of any project site also plays an important role and is also a part of the quality control of any project work. The HSE audits has to be conducted at the Project Sites for the various work areas generally encountered in the projects being executed

The following activities are to be maintained under HSE.

  • Display of Notices
  • Warning tape, lighting arrangement at location of any excavated trench, electrical installation or mechanical erection.
  • Fire Prevention and Protection
  • Environment & Health
  • House Keeping
  • Lighting
  • Noise and Vibration
  • Personnel protective equipment (PPE) for field staff
